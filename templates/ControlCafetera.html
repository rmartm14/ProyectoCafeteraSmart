Welcome to your smart coffee machine!

The main purpose of this coffee machine is to have your coffee done at the time you want. That's simple but very comfortable. So, the main work of it is selecting the time and filling the water and coffee deposit. If this two aren't filled, it will show you an alert to fill it. The filling of the water and coffee deposit is manual.
